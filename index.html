About The Club

Alnwick Town Juniors FC is a community-focused football club based in Alnwick, Northumberland. Our mission is to provide a safe, fun, and encouraging environment for children of all abilities to learn and enjoy playing football. We are proud to be an FA Chartered Standard club.
